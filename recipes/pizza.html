Low-Cal Pizza

Description

A weekly apperence in my household. This dish is perfect for satisfying those pizza craving without having to endure the bubbleguts.

Ingredients

  • Joe's Lavash Bread
  • Pizza sauce of your choice
  • Fat-free mozzerella cheese
  • Turkey pepperoni

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 415
  2. Lay out your Lavash bread on a cookie sheet
  3. Place a layer of pizza sauce, followed by layer of cheese, followed byt a layer of turkey pepperoni
  4. Place cookie sheet on the middle rack of preheated oven and cook for 12-15 mins depending on desired doneness
  5. Enjoy!
